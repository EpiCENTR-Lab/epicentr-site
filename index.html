EpiCENTR Lab

Airway epithelial stem cell biology

University College London

Research

The EpiCENTR lab studies the biology of airway epithelial stem cells and how epithelial–matrix interactions regulate tissue maintenance, repair, and disease.

Our work focuses on the molecular mechanisms governing basal stem cell adhesion to the basement membrane, particularly integrin signalling and laminin-332–dependent epithelial niche organisation.
