University College London

About

EpiCENTR studies airway epithelial stem cells and regenerative mechanisms in the respiratory system. Our work aims to understand epithelial repair, disease mechanisms, and regenerative therapeutic strategies.

Research Themes

Airway stem cells

Understanding the identity, regulation, and behaviour of airway epithelial stem cells.

Epithelial regeneration

Investigating how airway epithelia repair after injury and infection.

Disease modelling

Developing models of airway disease to inform regenerative therapies.
