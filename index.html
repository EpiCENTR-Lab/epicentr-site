EpiCENTR Lab

Airway epithelial stem cell biology

The EpiCENTR lab studies how airway epithelial stem cells build, maintain, and repair the respiratory epithelium.

We focus on basal stem cells and the molecular interactions that anchor them to the basement membrane, particularly integrin signalling and laminin-332-dependent epithelial niches.

Research

We investigate the cell biology of the airway epithelium, with particular emphasis on stem cell–matrix interactions, tissue maintenance, and repair.

Our work centres on the adhesion biology of basal epithelial stem cells, the composition of the epithelial basement membrane, and the mechanisms through which airway tissues maintain integrity and regenerate following injury.
